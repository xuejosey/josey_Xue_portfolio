Rough & elegant, quietly colliding.

Along the river, rough cobblestones and calm water exist side by side. 916 stretches this contrast into a freer visual language that drifts across each piece. At the border of classic and new, untamed spirit and careful craft start a slow, elegant conversation.

Rich knit structures shape warm, relaxed sweaters. Distinct textures build new volume and layers for shirts and trousers. Checks fall into clean sequences, while raw edges and paint‑washed finishes leave space for an unfinished story.
