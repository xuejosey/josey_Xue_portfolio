About

Goodbai, for the in‑between hours.

Goodbai Studio moves between rough streets and quiet rooms — clothes for when the city finally slows down and you can hear your own thoughts again.

916: riverside chapter

On the riverside, rough stone and calm water meet without effort. The 916 series grows out of this feeling — a small code that opens into a freer visual symbol, shifting across knit, shirting and wide‑leg trousers.

At the edge of classic and new, an untamed attitude and precise craft are having an elegant, ongoing conversation. The pieces stay relaxed, but the lines remain clean.

Material stories

Dense stitches draw warm, easy silhouettes for sweaters. Unique textures build new shapes and layers for shirts, re‑framing simple daily looks. On checked fabrics, stripes line up into firm sequences, while frayed hems and paint‑washed aging techniques leave space for an unfinished narrative.

Each piece is designed to be worn softly and repeatedly — something that feels better the more it lives with you.

Design notes

  • Unisex, wide silhouettes that move easily.
  • Neutral base tones with quiet, moody accents.
  • Details that only appear up close: stitching, texture, raw edges.
